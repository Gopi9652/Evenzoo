🧮 Value Score Formula

Value Score = (Quality Rating × 40%) + (Price Efficiency × 40%) + (Reviews × 20%)

Price Efficiency = (Budget − Vendor Total Cost) / Budget × 100

This sorts vendors so the customer always sees the best deal first — not who paid most to be listed.

🚗 Travel Cost Auto-Calculator

When vendor is from another district/state:

Travel Cost = Distance (km) × ₹12 (fuel) + ₹800/night stay if event is far

This is shown transparently on every card. Customer sees real final price before clicking.

📦 Bundle Detector

If customer needs Photography + Decoration, and two AP vendors are from same city, app auto-suggests:

"Book both from Vijayawada → share travel cost → save ₹5,000 extra"

No competitor does bundle logic across vendors.

📅 Availability Filter

Vendor marks which dates they are willing to travel to TS. Customer's selected date auto-filters only available vendors.

Prevents the #1 problem in cross-state bookings — vendor unavailability discovered after interest.

🧠 Core Algorithm — /search/vendors

1. Filter vendors by: category + willing_to_travel + availability on date
2. For each vendor: distance = haversine(vendor.lat/lng, customer.lat/lng)
3. travel_cost = distance × vendor.travel_charge_per_km + stay_cost_if_needed
4. total_cost = base_price + travel_cost
5. IF total_cost > budget → exclude vendor
6. price_efficiency = (budget − total_cost) / budget × 100
7. value_score = (quality_score × 0.4) + (price_efficiency × 0.4) + (avg_review × 0.2)
8. Sort by value_score DESC → return results

Business Model
How UtsavLink Makes Money
Multiple revenue streams. Start free to grow vendor base, monetize after trust is built.

💸 Booking Commission Start Here

Charge 5–8% on every completed booking. Razorpay handles escrow. Money releases after event = zero fraud risk.

₹30,000 booking → ₹1,500–2,400 per transaction

📋 Vendor Subscription Month 3+

Free tier: 3 bookings/month. Paid tier ₹799/month: unlimited bookings + featured listing + analytics dashboard.

50 vendors × ₹799 = ₹39,950/month recurring

🔝 Featured Placement Month 6+

Vendors pay ₹299–999 to appear at top of search results for specific districts on specific dates (like wedding season).

Works like Google Ads but for wedding season slots

📦 Bundle Commission Month 6+

When a customer books 2+ vendors through a bundle suggestion, charge extra 2% bundle fee. Customer still saves money overall.

Win-win: customer saves ₹5,000, you earn extra ₹600
